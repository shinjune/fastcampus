About Us

Flowspace provides on-demand warehousing for businesses. We handle the storage, transportation, and service of inventory on a month-to-month basis without space minimums. Customers have access to a nationwide network of warehouses through a single easy-to-use platform. Flowspace is headquarted in the nation's biggest industrial market, Southern California.
